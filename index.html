The world is in danger and the only ones who can save us are the Crystal Gems, but they're all trapped! Help Steven to free the gems so that they can fuse together and save us all! You will be given an energy crystal made up of a random number of gem shards at the start of the game. You need to collect the exact number of shards by clicking on The Crystal Gems who are each trapped in a Zircon block below. By clicking on a Crystal Gem you will get a specific amount of shards to add to your total. If you match your total shard count exactly to the random number of shards found in the energy crystal, you will free our heroes, allowing them to merge into Andromida and save the world! If you add too many shards to your total the crystal will explode and you lose! The number of shards given by clicking on each Gem is hidden until you click on it. Don't forget it! Oh, and the shard values change every time you start! Good luck! The Crystal Gems, and the world is counting on you!!
