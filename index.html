The world is in danger and the only ones who can save us are the Crystal Gems, but they're all trapped! Help Steven to free the gems so that they can fuse together and save us all! You will be given a random number of gem shards at the start of the game. The Crystal Gems are each trapped in zircon block below. By clicking on a Crystal Gem you will add a specific amount of shards to your gem shard total. If you match your total shard count exactly to the random number of shards given at the start, you will free the Crystal Gems, allowing them to merge into Andromida and save the world! If you add too many shards to your total you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
